About Iron Wood

Iron Wood is a local start up business that aims to express the creative minds of our community. We specialize in making custom furniture such as Epoxy tables, charcuterie boards, the possibility are endless. We offer 6 years of welding experience, Combined with 3 years of woodworking experience. My background started in the boat industry as an aluminum Tig welder. In the beginning of my career, I have obtained skills and continue to obtain skills to better server my community

Caleb and Janae Pederson married back in May of 2022. Caleb has gathered his skills through working as a welder in the boating and medical industry. Caleb has also picked up skills working in construction. Janae has been an artist for most of her life. Together they continue to pick up skills to better serve their community. Caleb and Janae are also very active in the fitness industry. They both enjoy competing in bodybuilding,powerlifting, and Spartan races. Both Caleb and Janae strive for excellence in everything they do.
